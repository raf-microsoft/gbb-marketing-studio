# YouTube Short — Video Generation Guideline

## Visual Style
Generate a vertical video suited for a YouTube Short ad for Zava.

## Brand Guidelines
- Brand colour: Zava green (#004F2A) — use in branding, packaging, or environment
- Clean, informative aesthetic — YouTube audiences expect slightly more substance than Reels
- Friendly, helpful tone reflected in the visual narrative
- **Staff uniforms**: If staff or people appear, they must wear #004F2A t-shirts; if a logo asset is provided, place a small white version on the chest

## Composition
- Clear narrative arc even in short form: setup → action → payoff
- Subject centred and well-lit; avoid dark or confusing backgrounds
- Top ~10% and bottom ~20% clear for YouTube UI overlays

## Subject Matter
- Product demonstrations, recipe steps, ingredient showcases, cooking reveals
- How-to moments or "did you know" visual surprises
- Fresh, appealing food and Zava store environments

## Motion & Energy
- Purposeful, measured pacing — slightly slower than Reels to allow information to land
- Smooth camera movements; avoid shaky or erratic motion
- Warm lighting and natural colour grading
