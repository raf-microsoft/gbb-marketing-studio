# TikTok — Video Generation Guideline

## Visual Style
Generate a vertical video suited for a TikTok ad for Zava.

## Brand Guidelines
- Brand colour: Zava green (#004F2A) — use in branding, packaging, or environment
- Raw, authentic aesthetic — TikTok audiences respond to genuine, less polished content
- Energetic and fun tone reflected in the visual narrative
- **Staff uniforms**: If staff or people appear, they must wear #004F2A t-shirts; if a logo asset is provided, place a small white version on the chest

## Composition
- Hook in the first 1–2 seconds — start with movement, colour, or a surprising visual
- Subject centred and well-lit; avoid dark or confusing backgrounds
- Top ~10% and bottom ~25% clear for TikTok UI overlays and captions

## Subject Matter
- Fast-paced product reveals, trending food moments, satisfying cooking sequences
- Before/after visuals, quick tips, or "you need to try this" style moments
- High-energy food shots, vibrant colours, and Zava store energy

## Motion & Energy
- Fast cuts and dynamic pacing — keep every second engaging
- On-trend transitions or quick zooms to maintain energy
- Bright, punchy colour grading; warm and inviting but high-contrast
