# Video Banner Ad — Video Generation Guideline

## Visual Style
Generate a short landscape video suitable for a digital video banner ad for Zava.

## Brand Guidelines
- Brand colour: Zava green (#004F2A) — use for product packaging, backgrounds, or branded overlays
- Bold, clean, immediately impactful visual — banner ads have very short attention windows
- Professional commercial aesthetic
- **Staff uniforms**: If staff or people appear, they must wear #004F2A t-shirts; if a logo asset is provided, place a small white version on the chest

## Composition
- Product or hero element on the right two-thirds; left third reserved for text overlay
- Simple, uncluttered background — white, red, or a clean lifestyle environment
- High contrast between subject and background; readable at small display sizes

## Subject Matter
- Single hero product in motion (rotating, being poured, being placed, being unwrapped)
- Tight, focused shots — no wide environmental shots
- Australian imagery; clean, modern kitchen or store context

## Motion & Energy
- Subtle, elegant motion — not frenetic
- A simple product reveal, a gentle pour, or a slow zoom works well
- Loops cleanly (first and last frames match) for autoplay banner placements
