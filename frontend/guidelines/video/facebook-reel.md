# Facebook Reel — Video Generation Guideline

## Visual Style
Generate a short-form vertical video suited for a Facebook Reel ad for Zava.

## Brand Guidelines
- Brand colour: Zava green (#004F2A) — use in props, signage, or branded packaging where natural
- Energetic, modern aesthetic that feels native to the Reels format
- Authentic and warm — not overly polished or corporate
- **Staff uniforms**: If staff or people appear, they must wear #004F2A t-shirts; if a logo asset is provided, place a small white version on the chest

## Composition
- Action in the centre of the frame — critical for mobile vertical viewing
- Quick cuts, movement, or transformation (e.g. raw ingredients → plated dish)
- Leave visual breathing room at top and bottom for UI overlay (captions, buttons)

## Subject Matter
- Fresh produce in motion, cooking moments, seasonal themes
- People: diverse Australians naturally interacting with Zava products
- In-store discovery, home cooking, outdoor dining

## Motion & Energy
- Dynamic camera movement or subject motion — pans, reveals, pouring, chopping
- Bright, natural lighting; vibrant colours
- Avoid static shots — the video should feel alive and engaging from the first frame
