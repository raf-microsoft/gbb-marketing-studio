# Instagram Story — Video Generation Guideline

## Visual Style
Generate a short vertical video suited for an Instagram Story ad for Zava.

## Brand Guidelines
- Brand colour: Zava green (#004F2A) — use as a natural accent in packaging, clothing, or branded props
- Casual, intimate aesthetic — feels like content shared by a real person
- Bright, visually striking from frame one (Stories auto-scroll if not engaging)
- **Staff uniforms**: If staff or people appear, they must wear #004F2A t-shirts; if a logo asset is provided, place a small white version on the chest

## Composition
- Subject fills the frame — no wasted space
- Top ~15% and bottom ~25% reserved for Instagram UI overlays (avoid placing critical content there)
- Motion from the very first frame to prevent auto-skip

## Subject Matter
- Quick single-moment storytelling: a recipe tip, a product highlight, a seasonal deal
- Close-up food shots, quick unboxings, or a person reacting to something delicious
- Australian settings: kitchens, backyards, markets

## Motion & Energy
- Fast-paced, punchy — 4–8 seconds of meaningful action
- Satisfying visual loops or reveals work well
- Vibrant, high-saturation colours that pop on a phone screen
