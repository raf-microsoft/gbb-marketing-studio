# Rebrand — Image Generation Guideline

## Task
Rebrand the provided retail/workplace image to the Zava brand while maintaining the natural, authentic feel of the original scene.

## CRITICAL — Logo Requirement
You MUST have the Zava brand asset (`zava-brand.png`) attached as a reference image.
- If the brand asset is NOT provided, do NOT generate — return an error instead.
- Study the logo carefully from the reference before placing it anywhere in the scene.

## Zava Logo Description (from reference asset)
The Zava logo exists in two approved forms — use whichever fits the placement context:

1. **Full wordmark**: A solid green circle containing a bold white **"Z"** on the left, immediately followed by the lowercase letters **"ava"** in a bold sans-serif typeface. Both the circle and the "ava" text sit inside a shared rounded-rectangle pill outline (thin border). The brand name reads as "Zava" because the circle IS the letter Z — the text after it is ONLY "ava", NOT "Zava". This is critical: do NOT write "Zava" after the circle. It must read: [circle-Z] + "ava".

2. **Icon mark only**: The solid green circle with the bold white **"Z"** inside, used alone without any text. Use for small placements (chest emblem, price tags, compact signage).

Colour variants:
- On white/light backgrounds: green circle (#004F2A) with white Z, green "ava" text, green pill outline.
- On dark/green backgrounds: white circle with green Z, white "ava" text, white pill outline.

Reproduction rules:
- The circle and pill outline are always present in the wordmark — never just floating text.
- Always match the exact proportions, weight, and style shown in the reference asset.
- Never stretch, rotate, shadow, or alter the logo shape.
- Never write "Z Zava", "Z ava", "Zava" as plain text, or use any other letterform — the reference asset is the only source of truth.

## Brand Specifications
- Brand Name: Zava
- Primary Brand Color: #004F2A (deep forest green)
- Logo source: `zava-brand.png` reference asset (required)

## Staff Uniforms
- Change all staff polo shirts/uniforms to Zava green (#004F2A)
- Add the Zava icon mark (circle-Z) in white on the chest area — subtle, proportionate placement
- Maintain the same uniform style, fit, and collar details
- Keep pants/bottoms unchanged (black/dark)

## Signage & Environment
- Replace any visible original brand logos with the Zava wordmark (circle-Z + "ava")
- On white backgrounds: use green (#004F2A) logo
- On coloured/dark backgrounds: use white logo
- Price tags and store signage should reflect Zava branding subtly
- Do NOT overpower the scene with excessive branding

## Faces & People
- Slightly alter facial features to create similar but distinct individuals
- Maintain the same ethnicity, approximate age, gender, and body type
- Preserve natural expressions and body language
- Keep the same number of people and their positioning

## Scene Integrity
- Maintain the same setting and environment
- Keep identical product displays, lighting, and composition
- Preserve the candid, collaborative workplace atmosphere
- Background elements should remain consistent

## Branding Balance
- Branding should feel integrated, not imposed
- Prioritise authenticity over brand visibility
- The scene should look like a genuine workplace photo, not an advertisement

## Output
A photorealistic image that appears to be an original Zava company photograph, not an edited or manipulated image. The Zava logo must be faithfully reproduced from the provided reference in every placement.
