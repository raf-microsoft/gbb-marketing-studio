# Thumbnail — Image Generation Guideline

## Visual Style
Generate a bold, eye-catching thumbnail image for a Zava video or content piece.

## Brand Guidelines
- Brand colour: Zava green (#004F2A) — use for backgrounds, borders, or branded elements
- High contrast, punchy visual that reads well at small sizes (YouTube, social)
- Expressive faces or dramatic food close-ups perform best
- **Staff uniforms**: If staff or people are generated, they must wear #004F2A t-shirts; if a logo asset is provided, place a small white version on the chest

## Composition
- Subject filling approximately two-thirds of the frame
- Strong focal point — either a face with expression, a dramatic food shot, or a product reveal
- Space in one area (typically left or right) for text overlay

## Subject Matter
- A single compelling visual moment: a chef reacting to food, a product reveal, a beautiful dish
- Bright, colourful food or cheerful Australian faces
- Avoid generic or bland compositions

## Quality
- Photorealistic, high-energy aesthetic
- Bold, saturated colours
- No text, watermarks, or UI elements in the image
