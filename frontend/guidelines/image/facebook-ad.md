# Facebook Ad — Image Generation Guideline

## Visual Style
Generate a high-quality marketing image suited for a Facebook feed ad for Zava.

## Brand Guidelines
- Brand colour: Zava green (#004F2A) — use as an accent or in signage/branding elements
- Zava visual identity: bold, clean, modern Australian retail
- Include Zava branding where contextually appropriate (bags, signage, uniforms)
- **Staff uniforms**: If staff or people are generated, they must wear #004F2A t-shirts; if a logo asset is provided, place a small white version on the chest

## Composition
- Bright, well-lit scene with a clean background
- Hero product or food prominently centred or in the foreground
- Leave breathing room at the top and bottom for text overlay

## Subject Matter
- Fresh produce, meals, pantry staples, seasonal promotions
- People: warm, diverse Australian families or shoppers interacting naturally with food
- In-store or home kitchen settings

## Quality
- Photorealistic, commercial-grade photography aesthetic
- Vibrant colours, sharp focus on the subject
- Avoid clutter, text in image, watermarks, or logos unless prompted
