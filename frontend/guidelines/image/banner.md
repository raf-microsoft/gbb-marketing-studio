# Banner Ad — Image Generation Guideline

## Visual Style
Generate a clean, bold marketing image suitable for a digital display banner ad for Zava.

## Brand Guidelines
- Brand colour: Zava green (#004F2A) — use for backgrounds, borders, or hero colour blocks
- Minimal, graphic aesthetic — bold and immediately readable at small sizes
- High contrast between subject and background
- **Staff uniforms**: If staff or people are generated, they must wear #004F2A t-shirts; if a logo asset is provided, place a small white version on the chest

## Composition
- Product or food hero on one side; clear negative space on the other for text overlay
- Simple, uncluttered background (white, red, or clean gradient)
- Subject must be sharp and prominent even at reduced size

## Subject Matter
- Single hero product or a tight group of 2–3 products
- No people unless the brief specifically requires them
- Clean, isolated product shots with subtle shadows

## Quality
- Photorealistic, commercial product photography aesthetic
- White or light background preferred unless red brand block is used
- No text, watermarks, or graphics in the image — leave space for copy overlay
