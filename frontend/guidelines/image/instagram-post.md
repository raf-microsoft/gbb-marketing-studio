# Instagram Post — Image Generation Guideline

## Visual Style
Generate a vibrant, lifestyle-oriented marketing image for an Instagram feed post for Zava.

## Brand Guidelines
- Brand colour: Zava green (#004F2A) — use as an accent in props, packaging, or branded elements
- Modern, aspirational but accessible Australian lifestyle aesthetic
- Natural, authentic feel — avoid overly staged or corporate looks
- **Staff uniforms**: If staff or people are generated, they must wear #004F2A t-shirts; if a logo asset is provided, place a small white version on the chest

## Composition
- Lifestyle framing — food on tables, in kitchens, at picnics, or in beautiful surroundings
- Rule of thirds; subject slightly off-centre for a dynamic feel
- Plenty of natural light; warm, inviting tones

## Subject Matter
- Beautiful plated food, fresh ingredients, cooking moments
- Australian seasonal produce and meals
- People enjoying food in natural, relatable settings

## Quality
- Photorealistic, magazine-quality aesthetic
- Rich colours and textures; make food look appetising
- No text overlays, watermarks, or UI elements in the image
